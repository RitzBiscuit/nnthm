We are on a mission to save our planet...

Project Elixir was founded by Ritvik Vuppala on the 8th of November 2021. He had a mission to save
the millions of endangered animals and to recover the Earth's beauty that it stored hundreds of years before.
We are spread across 6 continents and have rescue centers in over 60 countries. We are working with SpaceX and
National Geographic in order to start making a change for the better.




Meet Jumbo

Jumbo was stranded on a small island in the watering hole when the tide rose, he was cut off from his herd as was left without foos for days. That was before we came to his rescue and broght him to safety. We reunited him with his delighted mother and now he is our African Ambassador.

Patrick Star

Patrick Star was swimming for fish alone when he got lost and trapped ina cave, but luckily we were monitoring his waddle and we we able to fetch him just in time to save his life, now he follows our antartic exploration team around everywhere. You may have guessed that we named him patrik after the spongebob character that lives in a rock under the sea!

Snowy

When our chartographers where mapping out Canada's Northern tundra, they didn't notice snowy sneak into the car ride home. So when Stevey(The pack leader), opened the car boot, he got quite a shock to find snowy snuggled up by the binoculars. Ever since snowy would always stick around when we send teams up to the highly populated tundra, he even shows us some of his cool tricks.
