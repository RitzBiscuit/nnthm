Discover.

Asia








We can be found in every county in asia, where we have offices, scout camps and campaigns towards a better world. On the 09th of November 2021, we organisned a campaign in Russia on the ban of seal hunting. every day, about 20 seals are killed for their insulating skin inRussia and, this is rapidly worsening their position on the species list. Recently, we sent a letter to the Domestic Administration in China, protesting against the slaughter of shark species. After this, the Administration passed the law that shark hunting must be given permission to be allowed.

Africa



Our influence concerning african wildlife isn't that great, but I can tell you it is getting more influential every day. We are involed in nearly all aspects of the African wildlife. From savannahs in Kenya to deserts in Egypt. We are literally everywhere. On the 10th of November 2021, we artificially dug out a new water hole for the inhabitants of Serengeti National Park in Tanzania. This is now a safe haven for elephants and gazelles that visit the site everyday.

North America



In North America, we have only recently been given clearance to set up nesting sites for the decreasing population of bald eagles. Since that successful day the population has exploded. We have also been tracking downa cold, lost and frightened herd of bison who have been lost in the cold reaches of Canada. Only Yesterday we successfully led them out the wolf mountain valley and they are now safely on track to reach their home in Idaho.

South America



In the amazon rainforest, we have been saving countless animals from the dangerous forest fires and floods that occur there every day.

Austrailia



In Australia we have reunioted thousands of kangaroos, and rescued hundreds of sloths from a slow death.
